FAQ

Why is TravlrGuide different from any other Travelguide app?
TravlrGuide let you plan your trip yourself in advance using an intuitive webapp and exports this plan to your smartphone or tablet keeping in mind your personal prefferences. TravlrGuide is also a social platform that allows it for your friends to interact with your travel plans.
Is it possible to print out my Travl, because I don't have a smartphone or tablet?
Yes, this is possible, this feature is helas only available for globeTravlrs. Globetravlrs are allowed to create more then 5 Travls and many other rewards. GlobeTravlrs pay less then $2.00 a month. For more\ details see Pricing.
Is Travlrguide free and how does it stay free of charge?
Yes, Travlrguide is a free webapp that allows you to plan and share your Travls. I allows you to create up to 5 Travls and share them with friends. If you want to support TravlrGuide and make the most efficient use of it, feel free to upgrade to a GlobeTravlr account.
